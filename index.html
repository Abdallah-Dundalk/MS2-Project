One Minute to Midnight

Defeat the Enemy AI to Save Humanity

An anti-virus developed by DARPA has spontaneously become sentient and decided that Humanity is a virus and therefore must be erradicated. The Enemy AI has aquired the launch codes for the USA's nuclear weapons and has initiated the launch sequence bringing the dooms day clock to one minute to midnight. You have one minute to manually disable the launch and prevent armageddon. You must not fail, Humanity depends on you...

How to Play

This game consists of 9 buttons, each button surronded with two rings, an inner and outer ring. The goal of the game is to rotate each ring until it aligns with the direction indicated by the arrow on the the button at the center of the rings. Also, the ring that is aligned to the direction of the button, must match the colour. So if the button is red and pointing right, you must align the red ring so that its arrow is facing the buttons arrow.

You can only move one ring at a time. You must toggle between controlling the red or blue rings by pressing the toggle button which will chnage colour to indicate which colour ring you have activated and can now control. If the button is purple, you must align both the red and the blue ring to the buttons arrow. If both rings have the same alignment to the buttons arrow, but the buttons colour is not purple, the orientation of the rigns is not correct and no points are scored.

When the rings are correctly aligned, the button will change green and you will score 1000 pts. When all buttons are green, you have won. You must turn all buttons green before the timer runs out. You have 60 seconds to win the game. If the timer runs out, you lose. To play, simply press "Start" and have fun!
